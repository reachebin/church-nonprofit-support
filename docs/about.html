About

Practical website support with a project management mindset

I help churches, nonprofits, and businesses improve how they present themselves online and keep important digital work from falling behind.

Who I am

Clear structure, clean presentation, dependable follow-through

I work full-time in project management, and that strongly shapes how I approach website and digital support work. I like clean scope, organized execution, and outcomes that are useful in real day-to-day use.

That means the work is not only about design. It is also about clarity, consistency, and helping online content stay current without becoming heavy to maintain.

The best fit is usually with organizations that want a more polished digital presence and appreciate practical support rather than a large agency process.

What guides the work

Modern, simple, and useful

Modern presentation

Cleaner layouts, better spacing, stronger hierarchy, and a more current visual feel.

Low-maintenance structure

A site should feel polished without becoming difficult to update or costly to keep running.

Practical support

The value is in making real pages, updates, and recurring tasks easier to manage over time.

Best fit

Organizations this support suits best

The strongest fit is usually with organizations that need a stronger online presence, better upkeep, or more reliable follow-through on recurring digital work.

Support can include

  • Website refreshes and layout cleanup
  • Page updates and content organization
  • Announcements, events, and recurring uploads
  • Digital checklists and workflow cleanup
  • Simple support systems for repeatable tasks
  • Selected brochure-style website builds

Churches

A strong fit where events, ministries, announcements, or website updates need to feel clearer and more current.

Nonprofits

A strong fit where public trust, event information, and organized communication matter.

Local businesses

A strong fit for businesses that want a clean brochure-style site or a more polished digital presentation.
