About

Practical support with a project management mindset

I help churches, nonprofits, and community organizations improve website upkeep, communication consistency, and recurring operational follow-through in a clear and manageable way.

Who I am

Structured, dependable help for real day-to-day needs

I am a full-time Project Manager and have also taught Project Management at Lambton College. That background shapes how I work: clear scope, practical organization, steady communication, and follow-through on the details that matter.

This support is especially geared toward organizations that already have meaningful activity, but do not always have enough internal time to keep every update, announcement, and recurring task current.

The focus is not on making work feel bigger. It is on making it clearer, more consistent, and easier to manage.

What I help with

Support built around work that often falls behind

The strongest fit is usually with organizations that need a reliable hand with practical digital and operational work rather than a large outside agency relationship.

Website and public updates

Keeping pages, event information, service details, and public-facing content current and easy to trust.

Communication consistency

Bringing more order to announcements, bulletin or newsletter uploads, reminders, and digital posting tasks.

Recurring systems and follow-through

Supporting repeatable work with checklists, trackers, shared organization, and practical workflow improvements.

How I work

Simple, useful, and realistic

Clear scope first

Work is defined in a way that is understandable and manageable, so the first step feels useful instead of overwhelming.

Practical over abstract

The focus stays on real deliverables such as updates, posting support, checklists, trackers, and cleaner workflows.

Dependable follow-through

The value is not just in ideas. It is in helping important work actually move forward in a consistent way.

Best fit

Organizations this support is most suited to

The best fit is usually with churches, nonprofits, and community organizations that are active, stretched, and looking for steady practical help rather than a full-scale agency engagement.

Support can include

  • Website content updates and cleanup
  • Announcement and event posting support
  • Bulletin or newsletter uploads
  • Recurring admin checklists
  • Trackers, decision logs, and shared file organization
  • Light AI-enabled workflow support where appropriate

Churches and faith communities

Especially those that need steadier support for website updates, announcements, seasonal communication, and event information.

Nonprofits and community organizations

Especially smaller teams that need practical structure for recurring work and clearer public-facing communication.

Selected local organizations and businesses

Limited support may also be available where the work is simple, practical, and a strong fit for this model.
