Services

Current service structure

The business is currently being shaped around four core service areas, with churches and nonprofits as the primary focus and selected local small businesses as a secondary focus area.

01

Churches / Nonprofits

Operations Setup & AI Workflow Improvement Pack

A practical setup engagement designed to help organizations improve internal organization, recurring workflows, and day-to-day follow-through using simple systems and tools.

May include

  • Folder and file structure improvements
  • Action trackers and decision logs
  • Recurring admin checklists
  • Workflow review and practical improvement suggestions
  • Guidance on Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace use
  • Light AI-enabled workflow guidance where appropriate

Best fit for

  • Teams relying too much on memory
  • Recurring follow-through problems
  • Scattered documents and inconsistent processes
  • Volunteer-led organizations needing structure
02

Churches

Church Website & Communications Updates Support

Ongoing or periodic support for churches that need website content, bulletin-style updates, event information, and public-facing digital communication kept current.

May include

  • Website content updates
  • Event and service information updates
  • Bulletin, newsletter, or announcement uploads
  • Homepage refreshes
  • Content cleanup and consistency improvements

Best fit for

  • Churches with active ministries but limited admin bandwidth
  • Teams with outdated website content
  • Churches wanting more consistency in digital updates
03

Churches

Church Social Media Support

Practical support for churches that want more consistency in public updates, announcements, event reminders, and social posting rhythm.

May include

  • Announcement posts
  • Event reminder posts
  • Special service promotion
  • Basic content planning
  • Caption writing and post support

Best fit for

  • Churches already using Facebook or Instagram
  • Teams that want consistency but lack dedicated time
  • Churches with active events and communications needs
04

Selected local small businesses

Starter Website Setup

A simple website package for local businesses that rely mainly on Facebook or Instagram, or businesses that need a more professional online presence and clearer customer-facing information.

May include

  • Simple multi-section or small multi-page website
  • Mobile-friendly layout
  • Services and contact information
  • Links to Facebook and Instagram
  • Basic launch support

Best fit for

  • Businesses with no website
  • Businesses relying only on social media
  • Businesses wanting a more established online presence

Important note

Services are intended to stay practical and clearly scoped

Not a large-agency model

This business is intentionally focused on practical support and clear scope rather than broad marketing or full-service agency work.

Client fit matters

The best engagements are those where the work needed is clear, realistic, and genuinely useful for the organization.

Support can grow over time

A first engagement may be a simple setup or starter project, with ongoing support added later only if it makes sense.

Next step

Start with a simple conversation

A short conversation is the best place to understand whether there is a practical fit and what kind of support would be most useful.
