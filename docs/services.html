Services

Practical support built around real needs

Services are designed for local churches, nonprofits, and businesses that need useful support, stronger online presence, and clearer day-to-day structure without the cost and complexity of a large agency.

Current service structure

Clear service lanes with practical scope

The business is currently being shaped around focused offerings that are easier to understand, easier to implement, and better suited to organizations that want practical value.

For local churches and nonprofits

Operations Setup & AI Workflow Improvement Pack

A practical setup engagement designed to improve organization, simplify recurring work, and create clearer follow-through using simple systems and tools.

  • Folder and file structure improvements
  • Action trackers and decision logs
  • Recurring admin checklists
  • Workflow review and practical improvement suggestions
  • Guidance on Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace use
  • Light AI-enabled workflow guidance where appropriate
Best fit for organizations dealing with recurring follow-through problems, scattered documents, or inconsistent internal processes.
For local churches

Church Website & Communications Updates Support

Support for churches that need a dependable way to keep website content, events, announcements, and public-facing updates current.

  • Website content updates
  • Event and service information updates
  • Bulletin, newsletter, or announcement uploads
  • Homepage refreshes
  • Content cleanup and consistency improvements
Best fit for churches with active ministries but limited admin capacity or inconsistent digital updates.
For local churches

Church Social Media Support

Practical support for churches that want more consistency in public updates, announcements, event reminders, and social posting rhythm.

  • Announcement posts
  • Event reminder posts
  • Special service promotion
  • Basic content planning
  • Caption writing and post support
Best fit for churches already using Facebook or Instagram and wanting more regular, organized communication.
For local businesses

Starter Website Setup

A simple website package for local businesses that rely mainly on Facebook or Instagram, or businesses that need a more established and professional online presence.

  • Simple multi-section or small multi-page website
  • Mobile-friendly layout
  • Services and contact information
  • Links to Facebook and Instagram
  • Basic launch support
Best fit for businesses with no website, outdated online presence, or a need for a clearer customer-facing home online.

Important note

Services are intended to stay practical and clearly scoped

Not a large-agency model

The business is intentionally focused on practical support and clear scope rather than broad marketing or full-service agency work.

Client fit matters

The best engagements are those where the work needed is clear, realistic, and genuinely useful for the organization.

Support can grow over time

A first engagement may be a simple setup or starter project, with ongoing support added later only if it makes sense.

Next step

Start with a simple conversation

A short conversation is often the best place to understand what would be most useful and whether there is a strong practical fit.
